Here's a QGIS workflow to identify flood-prone areas in Kerala, using the provided data and algorithms:

**Objective:** Identify areas in Kerala that are susceptible to flooding based on low elevation, low slope, and proximity to rivers.

---
**Workflow Steps:**

1.  **Extract Kerala Administrative Boundary:**
    *   **Algorithm ID:** `native:extractbyattribute`
    *   **Parameters:**
        *   INPUT: `India Admin Level 1`
        *   FIELD: `NAME_1` (assuming the state name is in this field)
        *   OPERATOR: `=`
        *   VALUE: `Kerala`
        *   OUTPUT: `Kerala_Boundary` (vector)

2.  **Prepare Hydrology and DEM Data for Kerala:**
    *   **Algorithm ID:** `gdal:cliprasterbymasklayer`
    *   **Parameters:**
        *   INPUT: `Kerala DEM`
        *   MASK: `Kerala_Boundary`
        *   OUTPUT: `Kerala_DEM_Clipped` (raster)
    *   **Algorithm ID:** `gdal:clipvectorbypolygon`
    *   **Parameters:**
        *   INPUT: `HydroRIVERS South Asia`
        *   MASK: `Kerala_Boundary`
        *   OUTPUT: `Kerala_Rivers_Clipped` (vector)

3.  **Derive Flood Risk Factors (Slope, Low Elevation, River Proximity):**
    *   **Algorithm ID:** `native:slope`
    *   **Parameters:**
        *   INPUT: `Kerala_DEM_Clipped`
        *   OUTPUT: `Kerala_Slope` (raster)
    *   **Algorithm ID:** `native:buffer`
    *   **Parameters:**
        *   INPUT: `Kerala_Rivers_Clipped`
        *   DISTANCE: `500` (meters, example buffer distance)
        *   OUTPUT: `Kerala_Rivers_Buffer` (vector)

4.  **Rasterize River Proximity and Combine Factors:**
    *   **Algorithm ID:** `gdal:rasterize`
    *   **Parameters:**
        *   INPUT: `Kerala_Rivers_Buffer`
        *   INPUT_RASTER: `Kerala_DEM_Clipped` (for extent and resolution reference)
        *   BURN: `1`
        *   OUTPUT: `Kerala_Rivers_Buffer_Raster` (raster)
    *   **Algorithm ID:** `qgis:rastercalculator`
    *   **Parameters:**
        *   EXPRESSION: `"Kerala_DEM_Clipped@1" < 20 AND "Kerala_Slope@1" < 5 AND "Kerala_Rivers_Buffer_Raster@1" == 1` (This identifies areas with elevation below 20m, slope less than 5 degrees, AND within the river buffer. Adjust thresholds as needed.)
        *   OUTPUT: `Flood_Prone_Raster` (raster)

5.  **Polygonize Flood-Prone Areas:**
    *   **Algorithm ID:** `gdal:polygonize`
    *   **Parameters:**
        *   INPUT: `Flood_Prone_Raster`
        *   FIELD: `DN`
        *   OUTPUT: `Flood_Prone_Areas_Polygons` (vector)
